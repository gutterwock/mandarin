## [phase:atomic] 飞机 (fēijī) - Airplane
tags: transport, vocabulary, hsk1

**飞机** (fēijī) means "airplane" or "plane."

- **飞** (fēi) = fly
- **机** (jī) = machine

**Example:**
- 我坐飞机去北京。(Wǒ zuò fēijī qù Běijīng.) - I take a plane to Beijing.

The verb **坐** (zuò) means "to sit" but is used for taking transportation.

## [phase:complex] Transportation Methods Comparison
tags: transport, vocabulary, comparison

Different transportation methods are used for different purposes in Chinese:

| Vehicle | Chinese | When to use |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Plane | 飞机 (fēijī) | Long distances, going abroad |
| Train | 火车 (huǒchē) | Medium-long distances, between cities |
| Bus | 公共汽车 (gōnggòng qìchē) | Within city, cheaper option |
| Taxi | 出租车 (chūzūchē) | Convenience, door-to-door |

**Common verb with transport:** 坐 (zuò) - to take/ride

**Example:**
- 我坐火车去北京。(Wǒ zuò huǒchē qù Běijīng.) - I take the train to Beijing.

## [phase:complex] Expressing Location with 在 (zài)
tags: grammar, location, pattern

**在 (zài)** indicates location and means "to be at/in."

**Patterns:**
1. **[Subject] + 在 + [Place]** = [Subject] is at/in [Place]
   - 我在学校。(Wǒ zài xuéxiào.) - I am at school.

2. **[Place] + 在哪里？** = Where is [Place]?
   - 医院在哪里？(Yīyuàn zài nǎlǐ?) - Where is the hospital?

**Note:** 在 is used for both asking and stating location.

## [phase:integration] Transportation Decision-Making
tags: transport, communication, integrated, problem-solving

Different situations require different transportation choices. Consider these scenarios:

**Scenario A:** 你在上海。你去北京。
*You are in Shanghai. You go to Beijing.*
- Distance: ~1,300 km
- Options: 飞机 (2 hours), 火车 (4-6 hours)

**Scenario B:** 你在家。你去医院。医院很近。
*You are at home. You go to the hospital. The hospital is very close.*
- Distance: 2 km
- Options: 出租车 (10 minutes), 公共汽车 (20 minutes)

**Scenario C:** 你在学校。你去饭馆。饭馆在学校右边。
*You are at school. You go to a restaurant. The restaurant is on the right side of school.*
- Distance: 200 meters
- Options: 走路 (walk, 3 minutes), 出租车 (1 minute)
- *Note: 走路 (walk) is new vocabulary introduced here and is not part of HSK1 taught content in this subtopic.*
