## [phase:atomic] Time words come before verbs in Chinese
tags: word-order, sentence-structure

In Chinese, time expressions appear **before** the verb, not at the end of the sentence like in English. The pattern is: **Subject + Time + Verb + Object**.

Examples:
- 我**明天**去。(I go tomorrow.) — NOT 我去明天
- 他**今天**工作。(He works today.)
- 我们**现在**吃。(We eat now.)

Time words can also appear at the very beginning of the sentence, before the subject:
- **明天**我去。(Tomorrow I go.)
- **今天**他工作。(Today he works.)

Both positions are correct, but the time word must never come at the end.

## [phase:atomic] 早上 (zǎoshang) — early morning
tags: time-vocabulary, daily-routine

**早上** (zǎoshang) means "early morning" and refers to the time from dawn until about 8-9 AM.

Usage:
- 我**早上**喝咖啡。(I drink coffee in the morning.)
- **早上**好！(Good morning!)

The character 早 means "early" and 上 means "up/above," together forming "early morning."

## [phase:atomic] 上午 (shàngwǔ) — late morning/forenoon
tags: time-vocabulary, daily-routine

**上午** (shàngwǔ) refers to the late morning period, roughly from 9 AM to noon. It's the second part of the morning, after 早上.

Usage:
- 我**上午**工作。(I work in the late morning/forenoon.)
- **上午**十点 (10 o'clock in the morning)

The character 上 means "up/first" and 午 means "noon," together indicating the time leading up to noon.

## [phase:atomic] 先 (xiān) — first/before
tags: sequencing, time-order

**先** (xiān) means "first" or "before" and is used to indicate the first action in a sequence. It appears before the verb it modifies.

Usage:
- 我**先**吃饭。(I eat first.)
- 他**先**工作。(He works first.)

先 is often paired with 再 (then/after that) to show a clear sequence of two actions.

## [phase:atomic] 再 (zài) — then/after that (in sequence)
tags: sequencing, time-order

**再** (zài) in this context means "then" or "after that" when describing a sequence of actions. It appears before the second verb.

Usage:
- 我先吃饭，**再**看书。(I first eat, then read.)
- 他先工作，**再**休息。(He first works, then rests.)

Note: 再 has other meanings in different contexts, but in 先...再... patterns it indicates sequential actions.

## [phase:atomic] 然后 (ránhòu) — then/after that
tags: sequencing, time-order

**然后** (ránhòu) means "then" or "after that" and connects sequential actions or events. It's similar to 再 but slightly more formal and often used to connect longer sequences or clauses.

Usage:
- 我吃饭，**然后**看书。(I eat, then read.)
- 他工作，**然后**回家。(He works, then goes home.)

然后 can appear at the beginning of a new clause or between the subject and verb of the second action.

## [phase:complex] Describing a daily schedule
tags: daily-routine, time-expressions, integration

When describing a daily schedule in Chinese, combine time-of-day words with verbs to create a natural flow. Remember that time expressions come before verbs.

Example daily routine:
- 我**早上**七点起床。(I get up at 7 AM.)
- 我**上午**九点工作。(I work at 9 AM.)
- 我**中午**吃午饭。(I eat lunch at noon.)
- 我**下午**学习汉语。(I study Chinese in the afternoon.)
- 我**晚上**看电视。(I watch TV in the evening.)

You can also use 每天 to indicate daily habits:
- 我**每天早上**喝咖啡。(I drink coffee every morning.)

## [phase:complex] Sequencing multiple actions with 先...再... and 然后
tags: sequencing, complex-sentences

To describe a sequence of three or more actions, combine 先...再... with 然后 or use multiple 然后 connectors.

Patterns:
- **先 + Verb₁, 再 + Verb₂, 然后 + Verb₃**
- **先 + Verb₁, 然后 + Verb₂, 然后 + Verb₃**

Examples:
- 我**先**吃饭，**再**喝茶，**然后**看书。(I first eat, then drink tea, then read.)
- 他**先**起床，**然后**喝咖啡，**然后**去工作。(He first gets up, then drinks coffee, then goes to work.)

You can also add time expressions:
- 我早上**先**喝水，**再**吃早饭。(In the morning, I first drink water, then eat breakfast.)

## [phase:complex] Common errors with time expressions
tags: error-correction, word-order

Common mistakes learners make with time expressions:

❌ **Wrong:** 我去学校明天。(Time at the end)
✓ **Correct:** 我明天去学校。or 明天我去学校。

❌ **Wrong:** 我先吃饭和再看书。(Using 和 "and" instead of sequence markers)
✓ **Correct:** 我先吃饭，再看书。

❌ **Wrong:** 我再吃饭，先看书。(Reversed sequence)
✓ **Correct:** 我先看书，再吃饭。(I first read, then eat.)

❌ **Wrong:** 我每天晚上先然后看书。(Missing the first verb after 先)
✓ **Correct:** 我每天晚上先吃饭，然后看书。
